Placement Guidance

Resume tips, aptitude prep, DSA roadmap, and company-specific strategies.

Top MNCs

IT & Service Giants

  • TCS — Largest IT recruiter, 40,000+ campus hires/yr. TCS NQT exam.
  • Infosys — Infosys InfyTQ platform, strong fresher program.
  • Wipro — WILP program, service + product roles.
  • HCL — HCL Tech Bee for pre-final year.
  • IBM — IBM SkillsBuild, AI/Cloud focus.
  • Accenture — Massive fresher intake, ATAP program.
  • Cognizant — CTS GenC / GenC Pro tracks.
Core Roles

Engineering Specializations

  • VLSI Design — Qualcomm, Intel, Texas Instruments. Skills: Verilog, Cadence.
  • Embedded Systems — Bosch, NXP, STMicro. Skills: C, RTOS, ARM.
  • Power Systems — Siemens, ABB, BHEL. Skills: MATLAB, Simulink.
  • AutoCAD / CAD — L&T, Tata Motors, Mahindra. Skills: CATIA, SolidWorks.
  • Structural — L&T Construction, DLF. Skills: STAAD.Pro, Revit.
  • Process Engg — Reliance, ONGC. Skills: HAZOP, PFD, HYSYS.
FAANG Prep

Product Company Track

  • Google — 4-5 rounds; DSA + System Design. LC Hard level.
  • Amazon — Leadership Principles (STAR) + 2 DSA rounds.
  • Microsoft — Azure/GitHub teams, behavioral + coding rounds.
  • Meta — Coding + System Design + Behavioral (3-4 rounds).
  • Resources — LeetCode Top 150, Grokking SD, Striver Sheet.
  • Timeline — 6 months solid prep minimum. Mock on Pramp/Interviewing.io.
PSU / Govt

Public Sector & Research

  • GATE — Gateway for BHEL, NTPC, IOCL, PGCIL, GAIL + IIT/NIT MTech.
  • ESE/IES — UPSC Engineering Services for Civil Services roles.
  • ISRO — Written test + interview; Aerospace/ECE/CSE/MECH.
  • DRDO — CEPTAM/SET exam; Defence R&D labs across India.
  • BARC — OCES/DGFS via GATE; training + scientist role.
  • Study Plan — Start GATE prep by Year 3; use MadeEasy or NPTEL.
